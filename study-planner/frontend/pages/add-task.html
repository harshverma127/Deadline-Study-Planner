💡 Scheduling Tips

📅
Deadline matters most. Tasks with the nearest deadline get scheduled first.
🔢
Max 4 tasks/day. The planner avoids overloading you — tasks spread across days.
⏱
Estimated time helps the planner allocate the right number of time slots for your task.
🔴
Priority breaks ties. When two tasks share a deadline, High priority wins.
